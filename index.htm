How We Met

Long story short: Jill is John's former roommate's fiancee's sister.

Jill's sister, Beth, started dating Matt, John's roommate, in the summer of 2006 and soon afterward introduced us. We immediately hit it off. It may have been due to the fact I didn't think Jill would be interested in me (I thought she was way out of my league), so I was able to be myself.

Apparently, not trying to impress Jill impressed her. Later that night Matt pulled me aside and let me know Jill was smitten.

I was too, and we've been that way ever since.

The Proposal

At a family barbecue on Memorial Day 2007, I asked Jill to be my wife. Jill and I love spending time with our families, so the long holiday weekend was the perfect opportunity. Matt and Beth helped me organize an intimate cook-out at their place, and with the help of their daughter Ella, I popped the question.

While Jill was sitting in the middle of the living room, I handed Ella an earring box, and asked her to bring it to Jill. It took Ella less than a second to land in Jill's lap and shout "Auntie's earrings!" Whenever Jill and I saw a jewelry store she would mention how much she'd like new earrings, so I hoped getting some would throw her off my trail. It seemed to work - when she realized what Ella was holding, I said "That's not the right box." She turned around to see me on one knee holding an engagement ring box.

I asked, and she said "Yes!!"
